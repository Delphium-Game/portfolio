Who am I?

Liam Arbuckle is a full stack engineer passionate about building tools to help people understand and explore the world. After graduating from Perth Modern School in 2020, he took a break from university to focus on building his gaming startup, Signal Kinetics, which specialises in science-based multiplayer games. Along the way he has worked at companies of different sizes, including ShareSqair and Diversity Focus, and has competed in numerous programming competitions with his team, for example the Open Source Rover challenge and the Exploring Hell project. After receiving initial investment in his game, “Star Sailors” in 2021, Liam has juggled leading development of the metaverse game and his other role as a game developer at Aurorian Studios. Liam is excited and always on the lookout for new opportunities to improve his experience, knowledge, and contribute to exciting new projects in any capacity.
